# VitaMind Branding Guide

## Brand Name

**VitaMind**

## Meaning

-   **Vita** (Latin): Life
-   **Mind**: Intelligence

**VitaMind** represents an intelligent system designed to help people
manage and optimize their lives.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Vision

Create the world's most intelligent **AI Life Management System** that
helps individuals improve productivity, decision making, and life
balance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Mission

To empower people with AI tools that help them:

-   organize their life
-   make better decisions
-   achieve personal goals
-   live more intentionally

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Positioning

VitaMind is positioned as:

**"The AI Operating System for Your Life."**

Unlike traditional productivity apps that manage tasks only, VitaMind
connects:

-   tasks
-   goals
-   habits
-   schedule
-   insights

into a single AI-powered system.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Tagline

**VitaMind -- Intelligence for your life.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alternative Taglines

-   VitaMind -- Your AI life assistant
-   VitaMind -- Design your best life
-   VitaMind -- Think smarter. Live better.
-   VitaMind -- The AI operating system for life
-   VitaMind -- Your personal AI brain

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Personality

VitaMind should feel:

-   Intelligent
-   Calm
-   Supportive
-   Minimal
-   Future-focused

It should feel like a **trusted life advisor powered by AI**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Colors

Primary Color: Deep Blue #1E3A8A

Secondary Color: Electric Purple #7C3AED

Accent Color: Soft Cyan #22D3EE

These colors represent:

-   intelligence
-   clarity
-   technology
-   calmness

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Typography

Primary Font: Inter

Alternative Fonts: - SF Pro - Roboto

Typography should remain:

-   minimal
-   modern
-   readable

------------------------------------------------------------------------

-   intelligence
-   neural connections
-   life systems

Style inspiration:

-   modern
-   minimal
